Trade barriers are things that slow down or prevent a UK business from accessing an overseas market. They can affect businesses of all sizes.

Tell us if you think you’re facing a trade barrier. We’ll look into your problem and help you resolve it.

Report a barrier

Trade barriers are tariff, non-tariff or overseas direct investment restrictions that delay or block your exports.

Your goods might be facing a trade barrier if lengthy procedures delay them getting to market.

Your services might be facing a trade barrier if there are unnecessary charges that give an advantage to domestic suppliers.

You can read more about trade barriers.
